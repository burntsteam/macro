## Welcome to Macro

Macro is an integrated productivity platform where everything is connected through @links. Every text editing surface in the app works the same: @mention anything. Email, channels, docs, tasks, canvas, and files — all searchable, all interlinked. Here you will learn the onboarding process from signing up your team to managing your project.
