In Macro, you are not restricted to messaging only your team and anyone can be added to a channel by entering in their email. If users do not have a Macro account the will receive an email telling them about their channel notification.

As general best practices, channels should be well named and organized by topic. Notify the whole channel of your message by using @here or directly by using @user.

> To create a channel use shortcut <kbd>c</kbd> \+ <kbd>m</kbd>

You can also @Macro which will call an agent to address your message in a channel. @Macro can summarize previous chats, answer workspace questions, etc.
