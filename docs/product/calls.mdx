Macro calls are one of the core ways memory is updated and can keep up with your workspace. 

To start a call, use the button at the top of calls or enter the channel with the intended participants and  press the call button. Each person will hear a ringing noise when a call has been started.

> ### Important Call Sharing Information
>
> Every call a member of your team has is added to the calls tab whether or not you attended it. You can opt out of having a call added to your team file/memory but the default is to share it. When you opt out, the call will be added to your memory but not team level memory.

To opt out of a call being shared with the team uncheck the button in the bottom left of an active call.

Afterwards, it is auto-transcribed and an AI summarization will be listed after every call. A recording of the conversation and a readable transcription is always available at the bottom of the call's information.

From the calls tab you are able to ask AI to help with parsing through them including searching through transcripts and aiding in summarizations.
