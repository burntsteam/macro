Email is one of the core functionalities of Macro which is why it is highly recommended you link it at signup. In Macro you can triage your email normally while also using our Signal/Noise filtering and an agent to draft and send emails.

<Frame>
  ![Agent Email Draft](/images/agent_email_draft.png)
</Frame>

> To draft a new email use shortcut <kbd>c</kbd> \+ <kbd>e</kbd>

With multi email inbox, you can filter through multiple emails at the same time. To add another email into Macro, go to settings.

> Use <kbd>j</kbd> and <kbd>k</kbd> to toggle up and down through your emails. To archive emails and remove them from your email inbox, use shortcut <kbd>e</kbd>.

### Signal vs Noise

Macro splits email into signal and noise tabs. Signal is where all your high priority emails go, things like team member updates and customer feedback. Noise is for miscellaneous emails that are not crucial to your workflow such as newsletters and promotions.

For signal in Email, threads have to clear extra bars than the Macro inbox. They must be **important** and **not shared** with you by someone else. Routine or shared email won't be there.

You can change senders from signal to noise or vice versa at any time by right clicking on email thread.

### Sharing

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 03 At 11 52 56 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-03-at-11.52.56-AM.png)
</Frame>

You can forward, cc, bcc people as normal using Macro's email client or send an email thread inside of the app using the share button.

You can share an email inside of a new or existing channel and the participant(s) will receive  a notification on the left hand side panel.

Recipients of a shared email cannot reply but can view the entire thread. To reply, you must forward the recipient the email in Macro's email client.

### Mentioning

There are several ways to share in an email.

- If you @mention someone in an email, they get added as a recipient.
- If you @mention a document in an email, it gets added as a url link to Macro. The documents permissions also get changed so that anyone with the link will be able to view it.
